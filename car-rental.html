OUR CAR RENTAL ACTIVITIES

Rameswaram Travels is committed to keeping the pricing as simple and clear as possible. So, we provide one price for your taxi which covers a specified maximum number of kilometers and maximum number of days. So, you know upfront how much a taxi is going to cost you and plan your budget well.The prices are dynamic and transparent and give a win-win deal for both you and our partnered taxi service providers. You can choose a car as per your preference. To ensure safe and seamless transportation during our clients' travels, we provide options for tour vehicle bookings. From comfortable cars for solo travelers to spacious vans for families or groups, our vehicles are well-maintained and driven by experienced chauffeurs who know the local roads and routes.
