About Visakhapatnam

Visakhapatnam - Waltair

Visakhapatnam, Vizag, or Waltair is the financial capital of Andhra Pradesh. It is the administrative headquarters of Visakhapatnam district and the state headquarters of the Indian Coast Guard.Visakhapatnam is present amid the Eastern Ghats on the Bay of Bengal coastline. The beaches, hills, and valleys make it the most sought-after tourist spot. While visiting, go for darshan at some of the grand temples in Visakhapatnam. Most of the famous temples are within city limits, while others located a few miles away are reachable via vehicles.

Popular Places In Visakhapatnam

Here is the Visakhapatnam temple list. All of these temples have an essence of spirituality exuding from every nook and cranny. Take a look!

KATIKI WATERFALLS

Among the places to visit near Vizag for 3 days, the Katiki Waterfalls are an important tourist attraction. Formed from the Gosthani River, this waterfall descends 50 feet and is surrounded by lush greenery. Nature lovers visit this place for hiking and trekking, and it attracts tourists from all over.

BORRA CAVES

Among the best places to visit in Vizag, located in the Ananthagiri Hills in Araku Valley, are the Bora caves. These are the largest caves in the country, situated at a height of 705 meters. Discovered in 1807, this is a popular tourist attraction. The caves are made of karstic limestone that has carved giant structures and are exposed via natural skylight. It is surrounded by evergreen forests, a green landscape, and beautiful views.

SUBMARINE MUSEUM

The submarine museum is one of the top 10 places to see in Vizag. Situated within the naval submarine INS Kurusura, you can see this on Rushikonda Beach. The museum consists of artifacts that showcase the lives of warriors.

MATSYADARSHINI AQUARIUM

Located on the Ramakrishna beach, this aquarium has many varieties of fish. Kids love watching the clownfish, horn cowfish, and squirrelfish. Kids love to visit this aquarium and explore the interesting marine life and gather all the information about life underwater. This is one of the most visited aquariums in Vishakapatnam, loved by both kids and adults.

INDIRA GANDHI ZOOLOGICAL PARK

For wildlife lovers, the Indira Gandhi Zoological Park is a must-visit tourist place. This was set up in 1977 and is home to about 100 species of mammals, birds, and reptiles. The zoo is located inside lush greenery and vast acres of Kambalakonda reserve forest. This is the third-largest zoo, and it is named after the erstwhile Prime Minister of India, Indira Gandhi.

VUDA PARK

Spread across 37 acres of land, this park has 2500 trees and is the perfect place to take a quiet morning or evening walk with friends and family. This park has fitness centers and a kids play area that keep the locals and tourists busy.

DOLPHIN’S NOSE

This is the perfect place to get spectacular views of the city and its magnificent coastline. The Dolphin’s Nose has a large rock viewpoint that looks like the shape of a dolphin’s nose, so it gets that name. Tourists also come here to get a glimpse of beautiful sunrises and sunsets.

SIMHACHALAM TEMPLE

This is a temple for Lord Narasimha, which attracts tourists from all over. The Simhachalam temple draws a large number of travelers. The grand, majestic structure and the holy, sacred temple are the reasons people visit Vizag.

YARADA BEACH

If you are looking for top places to visit in Vizag, then the Yarada beach is one of the best places to see in Visakhapatnam. Yarada Beach has the Bay of Bengal on one side and magnificent hills on the other side. If you are looking for a relaxing beach vacation, then this is the place to be.

KAILASAGIRI

If you are looking for some of the best places to visit in Vizag city, then you must take a trip to Kailasagiri. Kailasagiri offers panoramic views of the city, forests, and beaches. Kailasagiri has huge Shiva and Parvathi idols. Devotees from far away come to see these giant structures of Lord Shiva and Parvathi. This is also a favorite spot for the photographers who love to shoot these giant structures with the forests as the backdrop.

RISHIKONDA BEACH

Nestled on the Bay of Bengal, Rishikonda Beach is the jewel of the East Coast. It is a beach with beautiful lush greenery, with Rishikonda Hill in the backyard. This is a famous place in Vizag, also known for water sports.
