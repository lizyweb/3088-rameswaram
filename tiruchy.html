About Tiruchy

Tiruchy - Rock Fort City

Tiruchirappalli is a famous industrial and educational city in Tamil Nadu. It is a place dotted with many temples, churches, and monuments. The scenic charm of the place makes it one of the most visited places in Tamil Nadu. The rich cultural heritage of the city can be seen through the architecture of the buildings in this city. Some of the most enthralling places to visit in Trichy include Rock Fort Temple, Railway Museum, Kallanai Dam, etc. Besides, Tiruchirappalli also has a few attractions, like Pachamalai Hills and Puliyancholai Falls, that make for an interesting day trip. It is truly a heaven for Hindu pilgrims as well as history buffs.

Popular Places In Tiruchy

Trichy is a place dotted with numerous religious and historical sites. Below are some of the best places to see in Tiruchirappalli Check them out along with other details like location and timings, and make your itinerary in advance for your next trip.

ROCKFORT TEMPLE

The Rockfort Temple is one of the top places to visit in Tiruchirappalli. It is an ancient fort, and its structure comprises large rocks. The Rockfort has three famous Hindu temples located inside its premises, namely the Thayumanavar Temple, the Manikka Vinayakar Temple, and the Ucchi Pillaiyar Temple. The striking architecture of this temple is one of the oldest constructions in Tamil Nadu and a must-see attraction.

SRI RANGANATHASWAMY TEMPLE

Situated in Srirangam Town, Trichy, Sri Ranganathaswamy Temple is one of the other famous places to visit in Trichy. The temple is dedicated to Lord Vishnu and is among the major Hindu temples in the country. It is built in the Dravidian style of architecture and is a must-visit for all Nirvana seekers and photo fanatics. The temple also has a royal temple tower and a hall where there are nearly 1,000 decorated pillars. Learning about them on a guided tour is one of the best things to do in Trichy.

JAMBUKESHWAR TEMPLE

Jambukeshwar Temple, also known as Thiruvanaikoil Temple, is one of the holiest places to see in Trichy. It is one of the five major temples dedicated to Lord Shiva in Tamil Nadu. The temples were built by Chola in the 2nd century AD in the Drvaidian style of architecture. It is one of the must-visit temples for anyone interested in the religious beliefs of the place or simply curious about the culture of Tamil Nadu.

KALLANAI DAM

Kallanai Dam is one of the most popular attractions in the city. It was built by Chola dynasty rulers and is nearly 2000 years old. The dam happens to be still in use and is located in a scenic environment in a lush green place. All those looking for some merry time with their close ones can visit this dam, as it is an ideal picnic spot. It happens to be a great place for history buffs and photo fanatics too.

ST JOSEPH’S CHURCH

Trichy houses several historic churches, and St. Joseph’s Church is one of them. Built in the year 1792 by Schwartz, it is one of the oldest churches in India and has seen many historical events related to colonial rule. The church was built during British rule when there were mass conversions to Christianity, and hence this church was used by Britishers to spread Christianity.

ARULMIGU MAARIYAMMAN KOVIL SAMAYAPURAM

Samayapuram Arulmigu Mariamman Temple is one of the most important religious sites in Trichy. As a matter of fact, the name Samayapuram reminds of Goddess Samayapurthal to Tamil people. This illustrious temple is located in the modest suburban area of Trichy called Samayapuram, just 16.8 km away from the city center of Trichy. The temple receives thousands of devotees on a weekly basis throughout the year, especially on Tuesdays, Fridays, and Sundays. Touted as the second most affluent temple in the southern state of Tamil Nadu, Samayapuram Mariamman Temple is a must-visit temple in Trichy.
